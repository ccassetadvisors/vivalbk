Why IV

Straight to the source

Oral supplements pass through your digestive system, where much of what you swallow never reaches your bloodstream. IV infusion delivers fluids and vitamins directly — absorption is complete and the effect is fast. Most clients feel the difference before the bag is finished.

Every visit starts with a quick health screening by your nurse. Your bag is mixed for you — never pre-made — and you're monitored throughout the infusion.

Full absorption, fast onset

Fluids and nutrients bypass digestion and go to work immediately.
